Project

"The Bluestone Sessions"
Acoustic Recording Sessions

2 Artists. 8 Songs. Epic Results.

In 2017, Country Rebel met Drake White and Aaron Lewis in Columbus, Ohio on separate occasions to film, what would later be called "The Bluestone Sessions." With cathedral ceilings, and soulful acoustics that can only be experienced in a 115-year old church, The Bluestone was the perfect location to record two of country music's most expressive artists.
