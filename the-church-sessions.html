"The Church Sessions"
Acoustic Recording Sessions

32 Artists, 4 Days.

Real. Raw. Country Music.

Country Rebel invited 32 artists out to the Nashville Vineyard Church in Nashville, Tennessee to record a collection of acoustic performances during Country Radio Seminar Week February 2019. Each artist recorded 3-5 acoustic originals and covers.
