Project

"The Church Sessions"
Acoustic Recording Sessions

32 Artists, 4 Days.

Country Rebel invited 32 artists out to the Nashville Vineyard Church in Nashville, Tennessee to record a collection of acoustic performances during Country Radio Seminar Week, February 2019. The team was able to capture around 120 individual song videos.

Click Above For Full Session Playlist

What I Worked On

From pre-production to post-production to delivery, I played an integral role in this project. As a producer/production manager, I purchased the necessary equipment needed and worked to secure any rental equipment that wasn’t on-hand for the project, as well as securing extra help. As a director, I made sure everything from the set, cameras, and lighting to data ingestion was running as smoothly as possible. As the onset audio engineer, I was tasked with getting the stage microphones positioned for optimal sound recording. I had to stay very organized in my head to make sure all aspects of my position were handled correctly while shooting with many moving pieces.

As the post-production supervisor, I was tasked with managing 2 editors in the completion of all deliverables for the project and preparing YouTube for the launch of the session. Required for that were different aspects of project management, quality assurance, and humility when getting feedback from the talent’s team.
